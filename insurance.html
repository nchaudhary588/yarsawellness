Insurance & Payment Options

Understanding how home care services may be paid for.

Yarsa Wellness helps clients and families understand private-pay options and long-term care insurance requirements before services begin.

How It Works

Steps for using long-term care insurance.

01

Contact your insurance company

Ask whether your policy covers non-medical home care and whether you may choose your own agency.

02

Confirm benefit eligibility

Ask about benefit limits, waiting periods, required assessments, and provider requirements.

03

Review payment procedures

Some insurers reimburse the client after payment, while others may allow direct payment to an approved provider.

Important Information

Insurance coverage is not guaranteed.

Every long-term care insurance policy is different. Eligibility, covered services, benefit amounts, waiting periods, and provider requirements are determined by the insurance company.

Yarsa Wellness cannot guarantee that an insurance company will approve coverage or reimbursement. Clients should confirm benefits directly with their insurance provider before services begin.
